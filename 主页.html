公司简介

环球物流国际运输私人有限公司，（以下简称环球物流）是经新加坡工商局注册、批准成立的綜合性的用际物流公司，公司注册号码201400940Z，业务范围主要致力于新加坡至中国，香港，台湾，马来西业，日本，美国，澳大利亚等国家和地区的空运快递及代购服务和中国至新加坡，马来西亚，孟加拉、泰国、越南，印尼，澳大利亚加拿大等国象的空运和海运及代购服务。

公司自成立之初一直本着“诚信做人，严谨做事，双贏做生意”的公司理念竭诚为新老套户服务，井且得到了很多老客户的认可和支持，感谢您的支持，让我们一起携手努力！

Global Logistics International Transportation Ple Ltd, (hereinalter referred to as Global Logistics) Is a comprehensive international logistics company registered and approved by the Singapare Industry and Commerce Bureau. The company registration number is 201400940Z. The business scope is mainly dedicated to Singapore to China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia, Japan, the United Stales, Australia, and other countries and regions. Air express and purchasing services and air and sea freight and purchasing services from China to Singapore, Malaysia, Bangladesh, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Australia, Canada and other countries.
